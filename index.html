information

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and unwind in the serene ambiance of Choffee. Indulge in a delightful fusion of Japanese aesthetics and aromatic coffee, We offer a harmonious blend of traditional Japanese elements and contemporary design. Step into a realm where every sip is a journey through the tranquil landscapes of Japan, and every corner whispers the essence of Zen.

our mission

Aims to create a peaceful and inviting space where customers can enjoy high-quality coffee in a setting inspired by traditional Japanese teahouses. With minimalist decor and cherry blossom accents, Choffee provides a serene atmosphere for guests to relax and connect. Through its commitment to craftsmanship and hospitality, Choffee strives to be a place where people can unwind, socialize, and experience the timeless charm of Japan.
